Post your day. Pixel-perfect.

How to Play

  1. Enter your P&L. Tap + WIN or - LOSS, then type the amount.
  2. Pick a template. Scroll the gallery and tap any pixel-art card.
  3. Add banners (optional). Tap one or more banners — numbers show the stack order, tap again to remove.
  4. Preview updates live on the canvas.
  5. Download PNG and post it to your trading chat.

Tip: nothing leaves your device — no signup, no upload. It's all rendered right in your browser.
